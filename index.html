Welcome to UnoReverse: A final project for SoftDes 2025

UNO is a classic card game beloved (or despised) by all! We wanted to challenge our own programming skills to make a virtual UNO 1v1 game where a player could practice their uno skills against a computer. Our biggest goal was to have a fully screen based interactive software that ran the game as officially as possible with visually appealing graphics and reliable rule amendment for our players.
